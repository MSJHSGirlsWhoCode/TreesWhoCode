Our Story :)

Tree Living hopes to provide useful information to plant enthusiasts. Our love for plants sprouted quickly, and we hope to spread that to others also. Some plants we love are cacti and flowers of all sorts!

Our Goals

Plants are essential in any environment, and today, pollution is a rising problem in today’s society. To target this problem, planting plants produces more oxygen, which is a small step to a safer, cleaner environment. We encourage individuals to grow plants, which can be aesthetically pleasing to any place and also good for the environment. Not only are plants a decorative piece in anyone’s home or space, we want to teach proper care in different types of plants. That’s why we have directions on every type of plant, so plant enthusiasts can keep their plants in good condition.
